# Hunter's Log

**Hunter's Log** is a World of Warcraft 3.3.5 hunter-focused addon that tracks your hunter career, pet history, kills, hunting grounds, trophies, bestiary progress, traps, achievements, and long-term pet memories.

It is designed especially for hunters who want their journey with their pets to feel more personal, persistent, and alive. (adds a button to mini map to bring up the Hunter's Log UI)

---

## Features

### Hunter Career Tracking

Hunter's Log keeps a persistent record of your hunter's adventure, including:

- Player kills
- Pet kills
- Player deaths
- Pet deaths
- Total damage dealt
- Highest crit
- Highest DPS fight
- Fastest kill
- Longest fight
- Distance traveled on foot
- Distance traveled mounted
- Arrows, bullets, and thrown weapons used

---

## Pet Tracking

Each pet gets its own saved history.

Tracked pet data includes:

- First time seen/tamed
- Total time spent with the pet
- Pet kills
- Pet deaths
- Best pet crit
- Best pet DPS
- Bond rank
- Personality
- Mood
- Dungeon/world/town time
- Stable-style memory card

Pets are meant to feel like long-term companions, not disposable combat units.

---

## Pet Bond System

Your pet slowly builds a bond with you as you travel, fight, and survive together.

Bond ranks include:

- Stranger
- Companion
- Trusted
- Bloodbound
- Legendary Partner

Bond progress is based on things like time summoned, kills, dungeon time, survival, and shared history.

---

## Pet Personality System

Pets can develop a personality based on how they are used.

Possible personalities include:

- Bloodthirsty
- Guardian
- Dungeon Beast
- Wanderer
- Town Lounger
- Survivor

Personality affects the flavor of your pet's random chatter and helps each pet feel unique.

---

## Pet Mood

Pets also have a current mood based on recent events.

Examples:

- Proud
- Wounded
- Restless
- Alert
- Content

Mood can change depending on pet kills, deaths, dungeon activity, idle time, and other events.

---

## Pet Chatter

Hunter's Log includes local-only pet flavor messages.

Your pet can talk or emote while:

- In town
- Out in the world
- Inside dungeons
- After kills
- During dangerous fights
- After major pet moments
